# ISI Nexus — Design System

## Color Palette
- **Background (primary):** slate-950 (#0f172a) — deep dark navy
- **Surface (cards/panels):** slate-900 (#0f172a) / slate-800 (#1e293b)
- **Accent (primary CTA, highlights):** cyan-500 (#06b6d4) / cyan-400 (#22d3ee)
- **Text primary:** white (#ffffff)
- **Text secondary:** slate-400 (#94a3b8)
- **Danger / Alert:** red-400 / red-500
- **Success / Safe:** emerald-500
- **Warning / Drift:** amber-400

## Typography
- Headlines: font-black, tight leading (leading-none / leading-tight)
- Section labels: text-xs font-bold tracking-widest uppercase — used as eyebrow labels in cyan or slate-400
- Body: text-sm or text-xs, leading-relaxed, slate-400 on dark / slate-600 on light
- Data values: font-black, large, white or cyan

## Component Style
- Cards: rounded-2xl, border border-slate-800, bg-slate-900
- Badges/pills: rounded-full, bg-cyan-500/20 text-cyan-400 or bg-slate-700 text-slate-300
- Buttons (primary): bg-cyan-500 text-white rounded-xl font-semibold
- Buttons (secondary/outline): border border-slate-700 text-slate-300 rounded-xl
- Navigation: dark sidebar or top nav, slate-950 background, cyan active indicator

## Dashboard Aesthetic
- Data-dense, monochromatic dark with colored severity indicators
- Metric cards with large number + label + trend indicator
- Log/table views with zebra rows in slate-900/slate-800
- Severity colors: red (critical), amber (warning), emerald (safe), cyan (governance event)

## Marketing Site
- Full-width dark hero with cyan gradient headline and badge ("World's First")
- Feature sections alternating dark/light or staying dark
- Architecture diagram section showing three-layer stack visually
- Pricing cards on dark background with cyan highlight on recommended tier
- "Why It's Worth Millions" section with risk cost framing

## Overall Aesthetic
Enterprise cybersecurity SaaS. Influence: Vercel + Cloudflare + Linear in dark mode. Not consumer. Not startup-flashy. Confident, precise, minimal.

## Anti-References
- No bright rainbow gradients
- No playful illustrations or emoji in UI elements
- No light pastel backgrounds
- No generic stock photography
- No generic startup-green or purple gradients
