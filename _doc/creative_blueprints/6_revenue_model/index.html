Business Model

Five ways to generate revenue from one system.

Stream 01

Subscriptions

Monthly and annual SaaS tiers. SMB to mid-market access to the ISI Platform dashboard and governance engine.

Monthly / Annual

Stream 02

Licensing

Per-deployment fees for companies embedding the ISI engine inside their own products or infrastructure.

Per Deployment

Stream 03

Enterprise Contracts

Custom annual agreements with large organizations. Tailored deployment, SLAs, and dedicated governance support.

Custom Annual

White-Label

ISI engine sold under the buyer's own brand. AI integrators and consultancies resell it as their own governance product.

Embedded Brand

Stream 05

Per-Seat Pricing

Expansion Revenue
