Foundation Validated

AI systems drift, hallucinate, and fail silently—exposing companies to undetected threats and corruption.

The Problem

AI systems operate as black boxes. One drifted model, one hallucination, one corrupt decision—and your security is compromised. Enterprises have no real-time visibility into model behavior, inter-model disagreement, or governance decisions. Silent failures expose companies to breaches, compliance violations, and undetected threats.

The Solution

Traditional cybersecurity treats AI as a black box. Enterprises lack visibility into model behavior, consensus, and governance decisions. One corrupted model or undetected drift can compromise entire security postures. Without real-time drift detection and multi-model validation, companies face data breaches, compliance violations, and operational blind spots.

Target Audience

Enterprise security teams, compliance officers, and security architects protecting mission-critical systems from AI-driven threats.

Personal Motivation

Enterprises deserve AI governance that works—transparent, accountable, and impossible to bypass. ISI makes AI security visible.
